Velankanni Park

Velankanni Park in Hosur is a popular neighborhood green space specifically designed for fitness enthusiasts and families. It is located in Hosured greenery with a variety of trees that provide ample shade, as well as a scenic view of a nearby lake. For children, the park offers a dedicated play area equipped with slides and swings, while adults can make use of the open-air exercise equipment. The park is free to enter and operates daily with split timings, typically opening from 5:00 AM to 9:00 AM and again from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM. Other basic amenities include cycling lanes, picnic tables, and public restrooms, making it a convenient location for short family outings or daily fitness routines.
