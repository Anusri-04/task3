Mala Kovil

Hosur Mala Kovil, also known as Chandira Choodeswarar Temple, is a famous and ancient hill temple located on the hilltop of Hosur in Tamil Nadu. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, this temple is believed to be several centuries old and holds great spiritual and cultural significance for devotees. The temple is situated at a scenic height, offering a peaceful atmosphere and a beautiful panoramic view of Hosur town and the surrounding greenery. Many devotees visit Mala Kovil early in the morning or during evenings, especially on Mondays, Pradosham days, and Maha Shivaratri, to offer prayers and seek blessings. The climb to the temple, either by steps or road, is considered sacred, and the calm environment makes it a popular place for meditation and relaxation.
